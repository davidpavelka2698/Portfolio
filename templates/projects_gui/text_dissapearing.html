Text Dissapearing

Play Against Time is a fun and fast-paced typing challenge. Start typing in the text box, but be quick! If you stop typing for 5 seconds, your text will disappear. The app features a simple interface with a 300x200 window and a large text box for typing. Your typing speed and consistency will be put to the test—keep the text flowing to avoid starting over. Enjoy the thrill of racing against the clock!
