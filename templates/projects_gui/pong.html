Pong

Multiplayer game that allows one player to move paddle up and down by "W" and "S". For second player the paddle is controlled by arrow UP and DOWN. Once bouncing ball gets behind other player's paddle, first one gets point and ball resets in the middle going in the direction of player that just got point. Game counts points for both players.
